Previous Career

For over 15 years, I was a quality control/assurance and R&D professional in manufacturing, warehouse, and laboratory operations. I've been on a management level for the past 6 years. As a Lean Six Sigma Master Black Belt, I've also done project consulting and professional training in project mangement, operational excellence, statistical analysis, experimental design, and quality management. I've worked with a variety of quality management schemes such as ISO, HACCP, BAP, GlobalGAP, AWS (American Welding Society), IDFB, and AZA. Additionally, I've been responsible for compliance with local, state, federal, and international governmental regulations.

Not only have I been a department manager with direct reports, but I've also been a project manager for several local and global projects with cross-functional teams. Projects have involved big data analysis for production improvement, designing and implementing quality management systems, engineering operational processes and quality testing methods, developing new products, conducting root cause analyses with corrective actions, and conducting scientific experiments. I've led or participated in projects spanning every inhabited continent.

In addition to working on projects that span the globe, I've also physically been stationed in the USA, China, and Norway. These experiences have given me extensive experience working with different cultures, personalities, and even languages. Working with diverse teams has always been a joy for me. Working in diverse organizations -- such as small businesses, non-profit organizations, and large multinational corporations -- in a variety of industries has also deepened my cultural experience and business knowledge.
